How it works

Four steps from estimate to finish.

Free in-home estimate

We walk the exterior with you, check siding and trim condition, and scope the work in writing.

Color consultation

Pick from our exterior swatch library or bring your own — we'll check HOA guidelines if you have them.

Wash, scrape & protect

Power wash, scrape, caulk, and prime first; landscaping and walkways get covered before anything is sprayed.

Paint & inspect

Two coats of weather-rated paint, applied by a two-person crew, with a final walkthrough before we call it done.
